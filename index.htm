Bin Fan received his BEng degree from Beijing University of Chemical Technology (BUCT) in 2006, and PhD degree from the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) in 2011. After got his doctoral degree, he had been worked in the NLPR, firstly as an Assistant Professor and then as an Associate Professor until 2020. He is now a full professor in University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB). During 2015-2016, he visited the CVLab at EPFL.

 

Bin Fan has wide research interests in Computer Vision, Pattern Recognition, and Deep Learning. His research is mainly focused on addressing problems related to applications of image classification, object detection, image segmentation, 3D reconstruction, image localization, 3D point cloud analysis, etc. He has published over 60 papers in highly ranked conferences and journals in the field, including IEEE TPAMI/TIP/TNNLS/TMM/TGRS, Pattern Recognition, CVPR, ICCV, AAAI, ECCV. He serves as an Associate Editor of Pattern Recognition (Elsevier), Journal of Visual Communication and Image Representation (Elsevier), Neurocomputing (Elsevier), Signal, Image and Video Processing (Springer), and The Visual Computer (Springer), AC of CVPR 2023/2024, ECCV 2024, ICME 20-24, SPC of IJCAI 2020/2021, AAAI 2024, and PCs for top computer vision and machine learning conferences (CVPR/ICCV/ECCV/ICML/NeurIPS/ICLR, etc). He is a Senior member of IEEE since 2016.
