Bin Fan received his BEng degree from Beijing University of Chemical Technology (BUCT) in 2006, and PhD degree from the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) in 2011 under supervision of Prof. Fuchao Wu. After got his doctoral degree, he had been worked in the NLPR, firstly as an Assistant Professor and then as an Associate Professor until Feb. 2020. Since Mar. 2020, he has been a full professor in University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB). During 2015-2016, he visited the CVLab at EPFL, working with Prof. Pascal Fua.

 

Bin Fan has wide research interests in Computer Vision, Pattern Recognition, and Deep Learning. His research is mainly focused on addressing problems related to applications of image classification, object detection, image segmentation, 3D reconstruction, image localization, 3D point cloud analysis, etc. He has published over 50 papers in highly ranked conferences and journals in the field, including IEEE TPAMI/TIP/TNNLS/TMM/TGRS, Pattern Recognition, CVPR, ICCV, AAAI, ECCV. He serves as an Associate Editor of Neurocomputing and The Visual Computer, SPC of IJCAI 2020, AC of ICME 2020, and PCs for ICCV/CVPR/ECCV/NeurIPS. He is a Senior member of IEEE since 2016, a recipient of ACM Beijing Rising Star Award in 2017.
